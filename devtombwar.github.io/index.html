    Variety of game systems, brand new battle system

    Other than its previous episodes, this time, real-time fight is adopted, players can enter the battle anytime, plus the character you control can be switched anytime as you wish. Want more? We introduce a new Party System where you can play with up to 5 friends in the same team!

    Multi-skills make the battle smoother than ever

    Skills can be equipped in for each character, they can be used anytime during the battle, making combats more diverse and smoother.

    Each character have their own Skills and you can make tones of combos with them.

    Once your character each a certain level, you will unlock an "Ultimate" Skill that will increase your gaming experience.

    Pet your Spirits

    Those little cute spirits can be available as the story goes deeper, they will assist you for better exploration along the journey, dont forget to feed them and play with them to get special rewards randomly.

    Mini Games in Game

    A card game and others 10 more games can be a better choice for you after the intense battle. This will also get you rewards and strong weapons and effects for the futures battles. You have more than 10 mini-games to enjoy.
